How to Use Iterate

  • 01 Click Generate or press Spacebar to receive a randomized design brief with a client, deliverable, target user, constraint, and time limit.
  • 02 Press C or click the copy icon to copy your current brief as formatted text.
  • 03 All previous briefs are saved in the Session Archive below. Use the search bar to find past prompts.
